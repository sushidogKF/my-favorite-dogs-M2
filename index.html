My Favorite Dogs

Hello, I really like dogs. Here is a list of my top 5 favorite dog breeds in no particular order.

1. Jack Russell Terrier

Small dogs packed with lots of energy. They were originally bred for fox hunting.

2. Chow Chow

Very fluffy dogs with a thick coat of fur. They are very independent despite their soft and cuddly appearance

3. Xoloitzcuintli

One of the world's oldest dog breeds. They are also called "Mexican Hairless Dogs" because of the way their coat grows so sparsely

4. Shiba Inu

Fox-like dogs hailing from Japan. They are independent but have a very sassy personality. They are known for having a loud scream for even the most minor of inconveniences.

5. Australian Shepherd

Very intelligent dogs that are known for their herding skills. They have a lot of variety in their coat colors. They are friendly and energetic for their size.

The More You Know

Did you know that all dogs no matter the breed, have a third eyelid? It is called the "nictitating membrane". It acts as a layer of protection, shielding their eyes from dirt and debris, almost like goggles!
